Welcometo Customized Dinning!

We feel that people don't really dine these days. From cafes to 5 course meals, dining is no longer about sitting down to eat and hence, butter beacons seek to provide you our dear diner with an experience where you will no longer be bothered by niusances like calling the waiter or trying to remember which dish you ordered last time that you really really loved. Now finally dining will be about what matters most. You!

Our Experience

Customer satisfaction is what we strive for. Through customization of Lighting and theme of your dining table to getting real time updates about your order through our status LEDs, we have kept your convinience in mind. But dining isn't only about consuming food. We also seek to empower restaurant owners and managers with real data about time delays, customer satisfaction and other essential preferences that people have at their restaurants and cafes. We cater to both sides of the table :)
